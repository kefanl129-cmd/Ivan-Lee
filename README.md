Introduction

This is not just a financial model.

It is an attempt to answer a deeper question:

What does it mean to make decisions under uncertainty?

By simulating three investment strategies with different risk structures, this project shows that:

The fastest path is not always the most sustainable
The highest return is not always the most attainable
And the biggest risk is often not volatility, but ruin

In a world driven by noise and short-term signals, this model highlights a quieter force:

Compounding, discipline, and survival.

Ultimately, every strategy is a choice:

Stability
Growth
Or volatility

And every choice reflects how we trade risk for possibility.
